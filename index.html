2024

pacifica'05

Pacifica'05 is a short film that I wrote and directed, with a release scheduled for May 2025. Made with the help of a skeleton crew of about five people, and a cast of three actors, it was three month long effort for pre-production and production, while post-production is currently ongoing.

The project was completed with the student organization Cinemadison, as part of our semester long short film initiatives. As part of it, the film was shot and made in the entirety of one semester, with the actual shoot taking the better part of a week. The goal was to come up with a film that could be submitted to film festivals, of which the submissions are presently ongoing.

WU Design Internship

The following are a selected sample of work that I worked on during a 2 year internship at the wisconsin union in the marketing and graphic design department. This included full usage of the adobe suite for motion graphics to vector art, as well as posters designed using Photoshop and Illustrator for both digital and print.
